// QUESTIONS

FAQ

Is this legal?
VoidReaper is designed for authorized security testing only. Every persona enforces ethical guidelines — they will refuse to help with unauthorized attacks. Always get written permission before testing networks you don't own.
Do I need internet for the AI?
No. Ollama runs 100% locally on your Pi 5 or your home workstation via Tailscale. Zero cloud dependencies, zero data leaks. Your scan results never leave your network.
What Pi 5 model do I need?
We recommend the 8GB model for running the AI models comfortably. The 4GB model works but limits you to smaller language models. For best performance, offload inference to a GPU workstation via Tailscale.
Can I create my own voice persona?
Absolutely. Order a Custom Voice (€20) and describe the personality you want — we'll craft the system prompt, tune the TTS voice parameters, and deliver a drop-in persona file. Or DIY it by editing vr-voice.sh.
What's included in the Software Pack?
The complete VoidReaper toolkit: 12-chapter HTML manual, all bash scripts (WiFi audit, web scan, recon, AI analysis, voice mode), 8 persona configurations, BlackArch setup guides, and Ollama/Piper TTS integration. Lifetime updates included.
